# Lists

---

## Ordered

### Markdown:

`1. first item`

`1. second item`

`1. third item`

### Output:

1. first item
1. second item
1. third item

---

### Markdown

`1. first item`

`1. second item`
  
  `1. first item`

  `1. second item`

  `1. third item`

`1. third item`

### Output

1. first item
1. second item
  1. first item
  1. second item
  1. third item
1. third item
